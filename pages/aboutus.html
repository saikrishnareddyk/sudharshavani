About Us

Sudarshanavani

      Sudarshanavani lead by a G.L.N.Swamy Garu, recipient of Vastu Ratna Award (Sir CV Raman Foundation - 2017) and Vastu Vibhushan (Suman Arts – 2023) is a Hereditary third generation Vastu Pandit engaging in providing Paripurna Vastu services from 24 years. Swamy Garu is offering a Vastu services by adhering to the principles of compass directions with degrees orientation by customizing the client needs and style without compromising on basic Vastu principles. Swamy Garu specializes in creating customized plans for homes, businesses, spiritual spaces, and schools for ensuring positive vibrations, peace and prosperity.

Sudarshanavani also provides Vastu services to an existing property / renovations / modification for supporting accomplishment of client vision / aspirations.

Swamy Garu with an objective to disseminate Vastu knowledge, continuously offering Vastu suggestions in many satellite channels like “I News, Om Spiritual Channel, Bhakti TV, Hindu Dharmam and Tori Radio-USA”. Also started YouTube Channel “Sudarshanavani” in 2017 to raise Vastu Awareness among people. The YouTube channel has over 1.66 lac subscribers and over 2900 videos providing educational awareness and clarifications on Vastu related matters.
